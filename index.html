Crimson and Cream Cupcakes was founded in 1821 and is Oregon's oldest boutique cupcake shop. We pour our blood, sweat and tears into every single cupcake we bake, sometimes literally.

Below you can find a gallery of our proudest creations. Be warned, do not linger too long on any of them, for when a man gazes too long into a cupcake, the cupcake gazes into the man
